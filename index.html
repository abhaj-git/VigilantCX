About me

A bright life is not one without shadow — it is one seen clearly.

Bright Life Astrology is an old soul’s practice: wisdom earned in ordinary life, offered without theatrics. I’ve walked the corporate tech world and kept returning to the same questions — why now, what pattern, what matters — the spiritual thread behind the schedule.

Clients come with real questions about love, work, family, and what comes next. In session we don’t nail down a fixed fate; we trace coherence: how this chapter rhymes with the longer story, so fear can ease and hope can find room.

Tarot speaks in images; Jyotish reads the sky you were born beneath; Bhrigu Nandi Nadi carries the thread of pattern in the lineage of the sages. The work stays grounded: luminous enough to see by, humble enough to trust your own choices.

Healing and right timing often arrive quietly, after honesty, rest, and one brave step. The light here does not demand perfection — it asks for truth. That’s the spirit of this work: precision with warmth, and space for you to move in your own time.
