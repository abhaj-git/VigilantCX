A space for honest seeing

I hold space for people who are carrying real questions: about love, work, family, timing, loss, and the next faithful step. Nothing here is about fear or theatrics. Tarot speaks in symbols; Jyotish maps the sky you were born under; Nadi carries the thread of karmic pattern in the tradition of the sages. Together they do not replace your agency—they help you listen more deeply to it.

When we sit together, we are not chasing a fixed fate. We are looking for coherence: how the moment you are in rhymes with the larger story, so that anxiety can loosen and hope can find a place to land. That is the heart of this practice—spiritual in the oldest sense: breathing room for the soul.

What I offer

Vedic astrology (Jyotish)

◇

Bhrigu Nandi Nadi

Consultations rooted in the Nadi stream attributed to Maharishi Bhrigu—exploring karmic threads and life direction with reverence for the tradition. Where it is appropriate, this can deepen perspective on purpose and passage.

Everything is going to be alright

Not because pain is denied, but because you are more than a single chapter. Healing and right timing often arrive quietly—after honesty, after rest, after one small brave choice. My role is to reflect the map with care, so you remember: the light does not ask you to be perfect. It asks you to stay true. In that truth, many people find the same gentle sentence on repeat: it will be alright. Not always on our schedule—yet alright all the same.
